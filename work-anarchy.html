Photo Anarchy

Role Graphic Designer
Tools Adobe Photoshop
Part of Multimedia Undegraduate Degree

In this graphic design project, the objective was to transform a visual concept using blending images and typography in Photoshop, and my chosen theme was the forest. Firstly, I conducted image research and selected various images that could represent or symbolize theme elements. Using Photoshop's blending techniques, I started by importing the chosen photos into different layers, then manipulated and adjusted each layer's opacity, blending modes, and masks to seamlessly merge them. Through doing so, I created a new composition and achieved a visually striking representation of the concept of a forest.
